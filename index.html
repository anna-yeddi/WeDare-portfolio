Currency Calculator

Currency Converter App helps users to convert USD into another usable currency (RUB, CAD, or EUR) in just a moment. Based on current market exchange rates, the app will calculate the currency's corresponding value of the entered amount of money. Under the hood, converter makes an AJAX request to live REST API and caches current market exchange rates of several currencies in JSON format to be dynamically presented to users at the page.
